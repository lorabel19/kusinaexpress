About KusinaExpress

Founded in 2020 in Quezon City, KusinaExpress delivers authentic Filipino flavors directly to your doorstep. From traditional Adobo to Kare-Kare, we bring comfort food closer to every Filipino home.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Our Mission

To provide fast, fresh, and flavorful Filipino meals that connect families to the taste of home through reliable delivery and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To become the most loved Filipino food delivery service nationwide, known for quality, reliability, and bringing communities together through food.

Our Values

Passion for authentic Filipino cuisine, commitment to customer satisfaction, and fostering community through shared culinary experiences.
