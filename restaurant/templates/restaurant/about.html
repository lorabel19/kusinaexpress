About KusinaExpress

Founded in 2020 in Quezon City, KusinaExpress delivers authentic Filipino flavors directly to your doorstep. From traditional Adobo to Kare-Kare, we bring comfort food closer to every Filipino home.

Our Mission

To provide fast, fresh, and flavorful Filipino meals that connect families to the taste of home.

Our Vision

To become the most loved Filipino food delivery service nationwide, known for quality, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our Values

Passion for food, commitment to customers, and fostering community through shared Filipino culinary experiences.
