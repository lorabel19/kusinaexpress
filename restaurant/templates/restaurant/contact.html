Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I place an order?

A: Go to the Menu page, choose your items, and click "Add to Cart". Then proceed to checkout for delivery or pickup.

Q: How can I track my delivery?

A: Use the "Order" section to view your delivery status and real-time tracking on the map.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept cash on delivery, debit/credit cards, GCash, Maya, and other popular e-wallets.

Q: Do you offer catering services?

A: Yes! Contact our catering team for special events, parties, and corporate functions.
